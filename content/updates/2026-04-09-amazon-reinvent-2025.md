In spirit of learning AWS for a certification at work, I decided to watch the last AWS Re:Invent conference. The entire thing was talking about 'AI Agents' and how much more they can do than simple chatting with or giving single orders. 

I must admit it's very impressive and I'll give it a try in my projects at some stage - although I can't see companies every truly replacing people with Agents so long as there's very real, very high responsibility over the sort of impacts systems running on AWS (at that scale) have on the world.
